## Getting Started

### Create Account

1. Go to app: https://marketprice.rw (or your domain)
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Select your role:
   - **Consumer**: Browse and compare prices
   - **Vendor**: Submit and manage prices
   - **Business**: View analytics
   - **Agent**: Collect prices from markets
   - **Admin**: Manage system
4. Fill in your details
5. Verify email/phone
6. Create password

### Login

1. Enter email
2. Enter password
3. Complete 2FA (if enabled)
4. Access dashboard

### Reset Password

1. Click "Forgot Password?"
2. Enter email address
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create new password

## Consumer Guide

### Browse Prices

**Find the Best Deals:**
1. Go to "Market Prices"
2. Select product (Tomato, Onion, etc.)
3. Choose market(s)
4. View all prices
5. Click price card for details

**Multi-Market Comparison:**
1. Click "Compare"
2. Select multiple markets
3. See side-by-side prices
4. View vendor information

### Price Trends

**Track Price Changes:**
1. Go to "Price Trends"
2. Select product and market
3. Choose time period (7, 30, 90 days)
4. See price chart
5. View trend direction (📈 up or 📉 down)

### Price Forecast

**Predict Future Prices:**
1. Go to "Forecast"
2. Select product
3. See AI prediction for next 7 days
4. View confidence percentage
5. Plan purchases better

### Create Alerts

**Get Notified of Price Changes:**
1. Go to "Alerts"
2. Click "Create Alert"
3. Pick product and target price
4. Choose notification method (Email/SMS)
5. Receive alert when price hits target

### Favorites

**Save Favorite Items:**
1. Click ⭐ on price card
2. Go to "Favorites" tab
3. Quick access to tracked items
4. Remove from favorites anytime

## Vendor Guide

### Submit Price

**Add Price to Market:**
1. Go to "Submit Price"
2. Fill form:
   - **Product**: Select from list
   - **Market**: Your market location  
   - **Price**: Per unit (RWF)
   - **Quantity**: Available stock
   - **Image**: Upload photo
   - **Notes**: Optional details
3. Click "Submit"
4. Wait for admin approval

**Status Tracking:**

## FAQ

### General

**Q: Is this app free?**
A: Yes, browsing market prices is free. Some premium features may require payment.

**Q: What languages are supported?**
A: English, Kinyarwanda, and French. Change in settings.

**Q: How do I change my profile?**
A: Go to Settings → Edit Profile to update name, email, phone.

### Pricing

**Q: How often are prices updated?**
A: Multiple times daily as vendors submit new prices.

**Q: How accurate are the predictions?**
A: Our AI achieves ~85% accuracy. Past performance doesn't guarantee future results.

**Q: Can I see historical prices?**
A: Yes, go to "Price Trends" to see history for any product.

### Submissions

**Q: Why was my price rejected?**
A: Check the rejection reason on your submission. Common reasons:
- Outlier price (too high/low)
- Incomplete information
- Duplicate submission
- Invalid data

**Q: How long does approval take?**
A: Usually 30 minutes to 2 hours during business hours.

**Q: Can I edit submitted prices?**
A: Only before approval. After approval, submit a new price.

### Alerts & Notifications

**Q: How do I enable SMS alerts?**
A: Settings → Notifications → Enable SMS → Add phone number

**Q: Can I get instant notifications?**
A: Yes, enable push notifications in your browser/app settings.

**Q: How do I unsubscribe from emails?**
A: Click "Unsubscribe" link in email footer or Settings → Email Preferences.

### Technical

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password?" on login, check email, follow link.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, encrypted end-to-end, industry-standard security.

**Q: Can I use the app offline?**
A: Limited features. Agents can collect prices offline (syncs when online).

**Q: How do I report a bug?**
A: Contact support@marketprice.rw or use in-app feedback.

### Account

**Q: How do I change my role?**
A: Contact admin. Role changes require verification.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: No, one account per person/business (terms of service).

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account (permanent).
